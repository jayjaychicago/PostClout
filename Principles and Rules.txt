Principle: Happiness is Play with others
Rule: Don't hoard resources from your team or make that your goal

Principle: It's okay to want personal rewards commensurate to your investment
Rule: Establish upfront the cost of your investment, the magnitude of the reward based on the magnitude of success

Principle: Don't be shortsighted
Rule: Employees should have a safety net to pay their housing and food and be incentivized to take big risks to have meaning in their life and the life of their team mates
